🏋️‍♂️ Program Overview

This comprehensive training program is designed to develop strength, agility, and baseball-specific skills for young athletes. Each exercise is carefully selected to improve performance on the field while ensuring proper form and safety.

💪 Strength Training

Build core strength and power for hitting and throwing

🏃 Agility Drills

Improve speed, quickness, and fielding ability

⚡ Combo Drills

Combine multiple skills for game-like scenarios

🧊 Cool-down

Proper recovery and flexibility maintenance

📋 Training Tips

  • • Always warm up properly before starting any exercise
  • • Focus on proper form over speed or weight
  • • Take rest days between intense training sessions
  • • Stay hydrated throughout your workout
  • • Listen to your body and don't push through pain
